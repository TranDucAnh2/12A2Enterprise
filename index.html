His enterprise

Cal AI

It is a mobile application, which uses AI for analysing images (food, drink, ingredients,..) made by users. By that, Cal AI can analyse the amount of calories in those..

This simplifies the way that users use for estimating the amount of calories in their food, drink,... Cal AI also tracks user’s eating pattern in order to offer personalized recommendations for nutritious, healthy diet
